## Overview of the Department
- Marketing & Sales manages paid channels for acquisition and retargeting.

## Process Steps
1. Brief and forecast (objective, budget, target CAC)
2. Creative and copy development
3. Build campaigns/ad sets with tracking
4. QA and approval (pixels, events, exclusions)
5. Launch and monitor daily
6. Weekly optimization and reporting

## KPIs / Quality Checks
- CAC, ROAS, CTR, CPM, conversion rate
- Pixel health, audience overlap, budget pacing
