## Overview of the Department

## Process Steps
1. Brief creation (owner, objective, target segment)
2. Copy and design draft
3. Build in ESP with links and tracking
4. QA checklist and approvals
5. Send scheduling and monitoring
6. Post-send review and learnings

## KPIs / Quality Checks
- Open rate, CTR, conversion, unsubscribe
- QA: links, personalization, segmentation, UTM accuracy
