## Process Steps
1. Triage and tag incoming tickets
2. Respond using macros/playbooks
3. Escalate per SLA and severity matrix
4. Resolve and log root causes
5. Update knowledge base and macros
6. Weekly review of trends and actions

## KPIs / Quality Checks

- SLA adherence by priority
