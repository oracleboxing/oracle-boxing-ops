## Overview of the Department
- Product & Coaching centralizes feedback intake and actions.

## Process Steps
1. Collect feedback (surveys, sessions, support)
2. Tag and categorize in tracking system
3. Prioritize and assign owners
4. Implement fixes/iterations
5. Close the loop with customers
6. Report trends monthly

## KPIs / Quality Checks
- Feedback response rate
- Time to action and close-the-loop rate
- Impact on satisfaction/retention
