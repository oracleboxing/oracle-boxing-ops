## Overview of the Department
- Product & Coaching owns curriculum content and update cadence.

## Process Steps
1. Identify update triggers (feedback, metrics, new standards)
2. Draft and review content changes
3. Produce assets (video, docs, downloads)
4. QA for accuracy, links, and accessibility
5. Publish and communicate release notes
6. Measure impact and log learnings

## KPIs / Quality Checks
- Release frequency and on-time delivery
- Content quality scores and error rate
- Engagement/retention impact post-update
