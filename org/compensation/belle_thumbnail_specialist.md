# Belle — Thumbnail Specialist

> Source of truth: [Compensation System Index](COMPENSATION_SYSTEM.md)

---

## Role Summary

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Role** | Thumbnail Specialist |
| **Department** | Content |
| **Primary Responsibility** | Design YouTube thumbnails for long-form YouTube videos |
| **Monthly Output** | 8 thumbnails (aligned with 8 YouTube uploads) |

---

## Base Pay

| Component | Amount | Cadence |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Per thumbnail delivered | £10 | Per thumbnail |

**Expected monthly base:** £80 (8 thumbnails × £10)

---

## Performance Bonus (Monthly)

**Metric:** Monthly average YouTube CTR across videos Belle created thumbnails for

**Measurement:** CTR measured 30 days after upload

| CTR Threshold | Bonus |
|---------------|-------|
| < 4.5% | £0 |
| 4.5% – 5.4% | £25 |
| 5.5% – 6.4% | £50 |
| ≥ 6.5% | £100 |

**Conditions:**
- Only videos where Belle created the thumbnail are included in calculation
- CTR is measured via YouTube Analytics
- Bonus is calculated and paid monthly

---

## Explicit Exclusions

Belle is **not responsible for**:

- Video titles
- Topic selection
- Upload timing
- Description copy
- Channel strategy

These remain owned by Content Creator and Content Manager roles.

---

## Rules & Governance

- Bonus calculated monthly after 30-day measurement window
- Bonus thresholds reviewed quarterly
- Thresholds may be adjusted by management based on channel performance and industry benchmarks
- Sustained underperformance (<4.5% CTR over multiple consecutive months) triggers a performance review

---

## Monthly Compensation Range

- **Minimum:** £80 (base only, 8 thumbnails delivered)
- **Typical:** £105–£130 (base + moderate CTR bonus)
- **Maximum:** £180 (base + top-tier CTR bonus)
