## 1-on-1 Coaching Sales Commission

| Component | Rate | Basis |
|-----------|------|-------|
| 1-on-1 coaching sales | 60% | Of all 1-on-1 coaching sales |

## Floor Guarantee

| Component | Amount | Condition |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Monthly floor | £1,000 | If commission total < £1,000, business tops up to £1,000 |

**Conditions:**
- Commission is calculated on collected revenue, net of refunds within agreed refund window
- This structure is outsource-ready

---

## Monthly Compensation Range

- **Minimum:** £1,000 (floor guarantee)
- **Typical:** £1,200–£2,000 (moderate sales activity)
- **Maximum potential:** Uncapped based on sales performance
