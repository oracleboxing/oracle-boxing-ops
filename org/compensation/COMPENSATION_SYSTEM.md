---

## Overview

Oracle Boxing uses four compensation modes:

| Mode | Purpose | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Flat** | Reliability and baseline commitment | Monthly retainer |
| **Unit** | Throughput and volume | Per-short payment |
| **Performance** | Outcomes controlled by the role | Watch Time bonus tiers |
| **Incentive/Bonus** | Milestone achievements | Constraint removal bounties |

All performance bonuses are defined with:
- **Metric**: What is measured
- **Measurement window**: When it's measured
- **Thresholds**: Tiered targets
- **Conditions**: Requirements to qualify

## Definitions

### AVD% (Average View Duration Percentage)
The percentage of a video's total length that viewers watch on average. Measured via YouTube Analytics.

**Example:** A 10-minute video with 4-minute average view duration = 40% AVD

### Watch Time (Hours)
Total hours viewers spend watching long-form content during the measurement period. Measured via YouTube Analytics.

### Meta Content Interactions
Total interactions (likes, comments, shares, saves) across Instagram and Facebook content for the measurement period. Measured via Meta Business Suite.

### Campaign Net Profit
Revenue directly attributable to a specific marketing campaign, minus all direct costs associated with that campaign.

### Refund Window
The period during which customers can request refunds. Commission and profit share calculations wait until this window closes.

## Administration

### Logging Requirements
- **Hourly work:** Log hours in shared tracker with description of work
- **Pre-approval:** Required for episodic hourly work (ops/finance)
- **Performance metrics:** Compiled and shared by end of month

### Disputes
Any compensation disputes should be raised within 7 days of payout.

### Review Cadence
This compensation system will be reviewed quarterly alongside performance reviews.
