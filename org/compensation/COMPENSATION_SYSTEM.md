# Compensation System

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-14
**Purpose:** Single source of truth for Oracle Boxing compensation structures, formulas, and performance metrics.

---

## Overview

Oracle Boxing uses four compensation modes:

| Mode | Purpose | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Flat** | Reliability and baseline commitment | Monthly retainer |
| **Unit** | Throughput and volume | Per-short payment |
| **Performance** | Outcomes controlled by the role | AVD% bonus tiers |
| **Incentive/Bonus** | Milestone achievements | Architect quarterly bonuses |

All performance bonuses are defined with:
- **Metric**: What is measured
- **Measurement window**: When it's measured
- **Thresholds**: Tiered targets
- **Conditions**: Requirements to qualify

## Jordan — Architect of Scale & Culture + Growth Operator + Operations Manager

### Growth Operator Flat Component
| Component | Amount | Cadence |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Growth Operator base | £1,000 | Monthly |

### Growth Operator Performance (Campaign Net Profit Share)

**Rate:** 20–30% of campaign net profit

**Campaign Net Profit Definition:**
```
Campaign Net Profit = Attributable Campaign Revenue
                    − Ad Spend
                    − Direct Fulfilment Costs
                    − Refunds Attributable to Campaign
```

**Components explained:**
- **Attributable Campaign Revenue:** Sales directly tied to the campaign
- **Ad Spend:** Meta ads, any paid promotion for the campaign
- **Direct Fulfilment Costs:** Any direct costs to deliver campaign promises
- **Refunds:** Refunds requested within the agreed refund window

### Challenge Upsell Commission
| Component | Rate | Basis |
|-----------|------|-------|
| Challenge upsell calls | 10% | Of collected revenue from challenge upsell sales |

**Conditions:**
- Commission is calculated on collected revenue, net of refunds within agreed refund window

## Definitions

### AVD% (Average View Duration Percentage)
The percentage of a video's total length that viewers watch on average. Measured via YouTube Analytics.

**Example:** A 10-minute video with 4-minute average view duration = 40% AVD

### Meta Content Interactions
Total interactions (likes, comments, shares, saves) across Instagram and Facebook content for the measurement period. Measured via Meta Business Suite.

### Campaign Net Profit
Revenue directly attributable to a specific marketing campaign, minus all direct costs associated with that campaign.

### Refund Window
The period during which customers can request refunds. Commission and profit share calculations wait until this window closes.
