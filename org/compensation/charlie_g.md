---

### Long-Form Performance Bonus

**Metric:** Monthly average AVD% (Average View Duration percentage) across all long-form uploads

| Threshold | Bonus |
|-----------|-------|
| ≥ 35% | £200 |
| ≥ 40% | £400 |
| ≥ 45% | £650 |
| ≥ 50% | £900 |

**Conditions:**
- All scheduled long-form videos must ship on time for that month
- Weekly insights report must be delivered
- Bonus is cumulative (hitting 40% earns £400, not £200 + £400)

---

### Monthly Compensation Range

- **Minimum:** £300 (flat only)
