# Growth (Marketing & Sales) Responsibilities

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-13
**Purpose:** Track the core responsibilities of the Growth department.

---

## Department Overview

Generates predictable customer acquisition and conversion through strategic marketing campaigns, optimised funnels, and effective sales execution.

---

## Responsibility List

| Responsibility | Owner | Cadence | Status/Metric |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Create campaign overview (theme, timing, angle) | Jordan | Per campaign | Overview documented |
| Write landing page copywriting | Jordan | Per campaign | Copy approved |
| Script and produce VSL | Jordan | Per campaign | VSL ready |
| Build landing page (front-end and back-end) | Jordan | Per campaign | Page functional |
| Define pricing and available packages | Jordan | Per campaign | Pricing finalised |
| Write email campaign sequence | Jordan | Per campaign | Emails scheduled |
| Script YouTube mid-roll advert | Jordan | Per campaign | Script approved |
| Liaise with Content to produce mid-roll | Jordan | Per campaign | Mid-roll produced |
| Update YouTube descriptions with campaign links | Jordan | During campaign | Links added |
| Create organic Instagram assets | Jordan | Per campaign | Assets scheduled |
| Create paid Meta ads assets | Jordan | Per campaign | Creatives ready |
| Launch and monitor Meta ads campaign | Jordan | During campaign | Performance tracked |
| Post-campaign debrief | Jordan | Per campaign | Learnings captured |
| Upsell challenge customers to membership | Jordan | During challenges | Conversion tracked |
| Identify 1-1 coaching prospects | Toni | Ongoing | Prospects identified |
| Handle pre-purchase enquiries | Toni | As needed | Enquiries answered |
| Close 1-1 coaching sales and collect payment | Toni | Per sale | Payment collected |

---

## Notes

- Jordan handles strategy and marketing execution
- Toni handles sales execution (1-1 coaching, enquiries)
- Campaign cycle: Plan → Build → Launch → Monitor → Debrief → Close
