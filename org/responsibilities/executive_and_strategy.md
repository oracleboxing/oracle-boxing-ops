# Executive & Strategy Responsibilities

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-13
**Purpose:** Track the core responsibilities of the Executive & Strategy department.

---

## Department Overview

Sets company direction, allocates resources, manages team performance, and maintains the culture and operating system that enables Oracle Boxing to grow.

---

## Responsibility List

| Responsibility | Owner | Cadence | Status/Metric |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Conduct quarterly budget allocation review | Jordan | Quarterly | Budget allocation updated and communicated |
| Set quarterly focus and key bets | Jordan | Quarterly | Focus documented |
| Update ops dashboard with priorities and status | Jordan | Weekly | Dashboard current |
| Conduct weekly planning session | Jordan | Weekly | Planning completed |
| Review department performance against KPIs | Jordan | Monthly | Reviews completed |
| Make hiring and resource allocation decisions | Jordan | As needed | Roadmap on track |
| Conduct performance reviews | Jordan | Quarterly | Feedback given |
| Update organisational blueprint as needed | Jordan | As needed | Blueprint updated when structure changes |
| Resolve cross-department conflicts or blockers | Jordan | As needed | Blockers resolved |
| Define and reinforce company culture and values | Jordan | Ongoing | Culture maintained |

---

## Notes

- Jordan is the sole person in this department
- This function enables all other departments to operate effectively
- Key output: Quarterly priorities, hiring roadmap, resource decisions
