# Product & Coaching Responsibilities

**Version:** 2.2
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-18
**Purpose:** Track the core responsibilities of the Product & Coaching department.

---

## Department Overview

Creates and delivers Oracle Boxing's core course content and live coaching experiences. Drives student results through curriculum and consistent coaching.

---

## Responsibility List

| Responsibility | Owner | Cadence | Status/Metric |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Create and maintain high quality course material | Oliver | Ongoing | Content current and high quality |
| Host 9 group coaching calls per week on Skool (no Zoom) | Oliver, Toni, Charlie | Weekly | 100% sessions delivered |
| Plan coaching calls at least 10 minutes in advance | All coaches | Per session | Prep completed |
| Publish coaching call recordings to Skool Call Recordings within 24 hours | All coaches | Per session | Recordings posted on time |
| Plan 3 core coaching topics weekly, shared across all coaches/time zones | All coaches | Weekly | Topics aligned |
| Run leaderboard rewards system (7-day Top 10 and 30-day leaderboard) | All coaches | Weekly/Monthly | Leaderboards maintained |
| Run weekly video submissions and accountability | Charlie | Weekly | Submissions reviewed |
| Deliver 1-1 sessions for paid coaching packages | Toni | 1-2 per week | Sessions delivered as purchased |
| Leave weekly video feedback on qualifying member posts | Charlie | Weekly | Feedback queue cleared |
| Deliver 15-minute onboarding call to every new member | Toni | As members join | 100% new members onboarded |
| Maintain satisfactory community presence | All coaches | Ongoing | Active engagement |

---

## Notes

- All group calls are hosted natively on Skool (no Zoom)
- All recordings go to Skool → Call Recordings (single canonical location)
- 1-1 coaching is primarily reserved for paid packages; leaderboard 1-1 rewards are limited and defined by weekly plan
- Fast-track 1:1 recordings are not posted to the community
- Use call and feedback logs to track completion and identify students needing follow-up
- Coach–Educator role consolidates previous "Community Coach" and "1-1 Coach" titles
- See department file for full coaching schedule with times
