Purpose: Outline responsibilities for Marketing & Sales to drive predictable growth.

## Department Overview
- Manages acquisition strategy, funnels, paid media, lifecycle comms, and premium coaching sales.

## Responsibility List
| Responsibility/Deliverable | Owner | Cadence | Status/Metric |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Write marketing emails and newsletters for all campaigns | Jordan | Weekly campaign rhythm | Weekly send calendar delivered with no misses |
| Build and optimise landing pages and funnels for courses, membership, and the 6-Week Challenge | Jordan | Ongoing with launch reviews | Active offers have live, QA'd funnels before launch reviews |
| Script sales videos and VSLs; create advertising assets (copy, graphics, thumbnails) | Jordan | Per campaign | Asset bundle completed before each campaign launch |
| Run and manage paid advertising campaigns (e.g., Facebook) | Jordan | Weekly optimisation | Spend and performance logged weekly with optimisations applied |
| Set up payment flows for upsells and cross-sells | Jordan | As offers launch | Payment links/flows live before offer go-live |
| Conduct mid-challenge upsell calls and handle pre-purchase enquiries/cancellations | Jordan | Weekly during challenges | Call log updated weekly with outcomes and objections |
| Close sales for the One-to-One Coaching Package and community memberships | Tony | Ongoing with challenge spikes | Close rate and revenue tracked per challenge cycle |

## Notes
- Capture conversion metrics per funnel stage and track upsell call outcomes for continuous optimisation.
