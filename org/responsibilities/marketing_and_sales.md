Purpose: Outline responsibilities for Marketing & Sales to drive predictable growth.

## Department Overview
- Manages acquisition strategy, funnels, paid media, lifecycle comms, and premium coaching sales.

## Responsibility List
- [ ] Write marketing emails and newsletters for all campaigns (Owner: Jordan; Cadence: campaign-based with weekly sends).
- [ ] Build and optimise landing pages and funnels for courses, membership, and the 6-Week Challenge (Owner: Jordan; Cadence: ongoing with launch reviews).
- [ ] Script sales videos and VSLs; create advertising assets (copy, graphics, thumbnails) (Owner: Jordan; Cadence: per campaign).
- [ ] Run and manage paid advertising campaigns (e.g., Facebook) (Owner: Jordan; Cadence: weekly optimisation).
- [ ] Set up payment flows for upsells and cross-sells (Owner: Jordan; Cadence: as offers launch).
- [ ] Conduct mid-challenge upsell calls and handle pre-purchase enquiries/cancellations (Owner: Jordan; Cadence: weekly during challenges).
- [ ] Close sales for the One-to-One Coaching Package and community memberships (Owner: Tony; Cadence: ongoing with challenge spikes).

## Notes
- Capture conversion metrics per funnel stage and track upsell call outcomes for continuous optimisation.
