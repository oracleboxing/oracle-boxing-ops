# Content & Media Responsibilities
Version: 1.1
Last Updated: 2025-05-08
Purpose: Define responsibilities for the Content & Media department.

---
## Department Overview
- Produces public-facing media (YouTube, vlogs, and social clips) independent of the course curriculum.

## Responsibility List
| Responsibility/Deliverable | Owner | Cadence | Status/Metric |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Script and film two YouTube videos plus a weekly vlog | Ollie | Weekly | 2 long-form releases and 1 vlog published each week |
| Produce 3–4 short-form social videos | Ollie | Weekly | 3–4 shorts delivered and scheduled weekly |
| Edit long-form and short-form content to publication-ready quality | G | Weekly release schedule | Edits delivered on time with no publishing delays |
| Repurpose long-form footage into shorts and clips | Ollie/G | Weekly | Long-form projects yield clipped assets each week |
| Maintain consistent branding across all media assets | Ollie/G | Ongoing | Branding checklist applied with no deviations in weekly outputs |

## Notes
- Keep an asset tracker that links scripts, raw footage, edits, and published links for each piece.
