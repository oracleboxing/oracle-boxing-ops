# Marketing & Sales Lead
Version: 1.0
Last Updated: 2025-05-06
Purpose: Capture the scope and measures for the Marketing & Sales leadership role.

---
## Department Overview
- Owns demand generation, funnels, paid media, and enrollment/sales motions.

## Role Overview
- Plans and executes campaigns, optimizes funnels, and runs sales operations.

## Key Responsibilities (List Template)
- [ ] Build and optimize funnels and landing pages
- [ ] Manage paid media strategy and spend
- [ ] Oversee email/lifecycle nurture
- [ ] Lead sales process and pipeline management
- [ ] Align with product and content on launch messaging

## KPIs / Success Measures
- CAC/LTV balance and payback
- Conversion rates across funnel stages
- Pipeline velocity and close rate
