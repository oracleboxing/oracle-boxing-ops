## Scope

Set company vision, strategy, and culture. Allocate resources across departments. Own hiring decisions and team performance. Maintain the operating system (blueprint, dashboard, deliverables) that enables the team to execute.

Currently also holding **Growth Operator** and **Operations Manager** roles until those can be delegated.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### Strategic Leadership
- [ ] Set and communicate company vision and quarterly priorities
- [ ] Conduct quarterly organisational blueprint review
- [ ] Define annual budget and capital allocation
- [ ] Make resource allocation decisions across departments

### People & Culture
- [ ] Own hiring decisions and team composition
- [ ] Conduct quarterly performance reviews
- [ ] Define and reinforce company culture and values
- [ ] Resolve cross-department conflicts or blockers

### Operational Oversight
- [ ] Update ops dashboard with weekly priorities and status
- [ ] Conduct weekly planning session
- [ ] Review department performance against KPIs (monthly)

### Stakeholder Management
- [ ] Manage key partnerships and external relationships
- [ ] Handle major customer escalations if needed
- [ ] Make final decisions on significant investments

---

## Decision Rights

### Decides Without Asking
- Quarterly and annual priorities
- Hiring and firing decisions
- Budget allocation within approved limits
- Operational policy and process changes
- Contractor engagement and SOW approval

### Must Escalate / Consult
- Major strategic pivots (self-reflection / advisors)
- Significant financial commitments (>£X threshold)
- Legal or compliance matters (legal counsel)

---

## KPIs / Success Measures

- **Revenue growth:** Year-over-year growth target
- **Profit margin:** Healthy margin maintained
- **Retention rate:** Member retention within target
- **Strategic milestones:** Quarterly priorities achieved
- **Team health:** Performance reviews completed, hiring roadmap on track
- **Operating system:** Blueprint, dashboard, and deliverables current and useful
