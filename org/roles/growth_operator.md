## Scope

Own customer acquisition and conversion systems: campaign strategy, funnels, landing pages, email marketing, paid advertising, and offer design. Drive predictable growth through systematic marketing execution.

Jordan currently holds this role alongside CEO and Operations Manager. Mid-term goal is to delegate to a dedicated hire.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### Campaign Strategy
- [ ] Plan marketing campaigns (theme, timing, duration, angle)
- [ ] Define pricing and package structure for each campaign
- [ ] Coordinate campaign timeline with Content and Operations
- [ ] Conduct post-campaign debriefs and capture learnings

### Funnel & Landing Pages
- [ ] Write landing page copywriting
- [ ] Script and produce video sales letters (VSLs)
- [ ] Build landing page front-end (design, responsiveness)
- [ ] Build landing page back-end (forms, integrations, tracking)

### Email Marketing
- [ ] Write email campaign sequences per offer
- [ ] Send regular newsletters to nurture audience
- [ ] Manage email list health and segmentation
- [ ] Track email performance (open rates, click rates)

### Content Integration
- [ ] Script YouTube mid-roll adverts for campaigns
- [ ] Liaise with Content team to produce mid-rolls
- [ ] Update YouTube descriptions with campaign links (temporary)
- [ ] Remove mid-rolls and links when campaigns close

### Paid Advertising
- [ ] Create paid Meta ads marketing assets
- [ ] Schedule and launch Meta ads campaigns
- [ ] Monitor campaign performance daily during campaigns
- [ ] Optimise ads based on performance data

### Organic Social
- [ ] Create organic Instagram assets (reels, carousels, stories)
- [ ] Maintain social presence during campaigns
- [ ] Coordinate with Content on organic reach

### Challenge Upsells
- [ ] Upsell challenge customers to continuity via check-in calls
- [ ] Track upsell conversion rates
- [ ] Optimise upsell approach based on data

---

## Decision Rights

### Decides Without Asking
- Campaign themes and creative approach
- Landing page copy and design
- Email sequence structure and timing
- Ad creative and targeting (within budget)
- Pricing within approved ranges

### Must Escalate
- New product/offer creation (CEO)
- Significant budget increases (CEO)
- Major pricing changes (CEO)
- Strategic partnerships or affiliates (CEO)

---

## KPIs / Success Measures

- **Campaign revenue:** Revenue generated per campaign
- **Conversion rate:** Landing page visitors → customers
- **Email metrics:** Open rate, click rate, list growth
- **Ad ROAS:** Return on ad spend
- **Upsell conversion:** Challenge → membership conversion rate
- **Cost per acquisition:** Efficient customer acquisition
