# Operations & Finance Lead
Version: 1.1
Last Updated: 2025-05-07
Purpose: Define the Operations & Finance role, scope, and metrics.

---
## Department Overview
- Ensures reliable systems, support, automations, billing, and contractor payments for Oracle Boxing.

## Role Overview
- Jordan: Head of Operations & Finance; manages support inbox, automations (Make.com/Stripe), site and portal uptime, payment processing, and contractor payments; oversees ad-hoc merchandise operations.

## Key Responsibilities
- Answer and resolve customer support emails (cancellations, access issues, general queries) with logged outcomes.
- Maintain and troubleshoot Make.com and Stripe automations; keep incident logs.
- Keep websites and membership portals functional; schedule minimal updates as required.
- Manage payment processing, reconciliations, and contractor payments on time.
- Oversee project-based merchandise operations when active.

## KPIs / Success Measures
- Support SLA adherence and resolution time for tickets.
- Automation uptime and incident recovery time.
- Site/portal availability and payment success rates.
- On-time contractor payments and reconciliation completeness.

## Notes
- Maintain a weekly reliability report summarising support volume, automation incidents, and payment health.
