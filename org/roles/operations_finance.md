# Operations & Finance Lead
Version: 1.0
Last Updated: 2025-05-06
Purpose: Define the Operations & Finance leadership role, scope, and metrics.

---
## Department Overview
- Ensures reliable systems, support, compliance, and financial stewardship.

## Role Overview
- Manages operations stack, support workflows, financial processes, and compliance.

## Key Responsibilities (List Template)
- [ ] Maintain automations and operational tooling
- [ ] Oversee support SLAs and escalations
- [ ] Run invoicing, payroll, VAT, and reconciliations
- [ ] Manage vendor contracts and data security
- [ ] Report on cashflow and operational risk

## KPIs / Success Measures
- SLA adherence and incident rates
- On-time close, payroll, and compliance filings
- Cashflow health and cost control
