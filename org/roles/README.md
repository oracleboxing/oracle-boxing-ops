- **Update when needed:** Update role files when scope or responsibilities change
- **Keep aligned:** Ensure responsibilities align with departmental objectives

---

## Role File Template

Each role file includes:

1. **Role Summary** – Holder, department, reporting line
2. **Scope** – What the role owns and is accountable for
3. **Key Responsibilities** – Specific duties and deliverables
4. **Decision Rights** – What they can decide vs. must escalate
5. **KPIs / Success Measures** – How success is measured
6. **Interfaces** – Who they collaborate with and how often
7. **Notes** – Additional context and considerations
