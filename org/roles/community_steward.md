**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-13
**Purpose:** Define the Community Steward role scope, decision rights, and success measures for Oracle Boxing.

---

| Attribute | Value |
| --- | --- |

---

## Scope

Own community health, member retention, and accountability systems. Proactively engage with members, capture success stories, and identify at-risk members before they churn. Free Coach–Educators to focus on coaching delivery.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### Daily Community Presence
- [ ] Maintain active presence in community (comments, likes, encouragement)
- [ ] Respond to member questions and posts within 24 hours
- [ ] Welcome new members and help them get oriented
- [ ] Model positive community behaviour and culture

### Accountability System
- [ ] Run weekly accountability check-ins with members
- [ ] Follow up with members who miss commitments
- [ ] Maintain accountability tracker/dashboard
- [ ] Celebrate member wins and progress

### Success Story Capture
- [ ] Identify members achieving notable results
- [ ] Document success stories (before/after, testimonial, permission)
- [ ] Coordinate with Growth for case study creation
- [ ] Maintain success story library

### Churn Prevention
- [ ] Monitor engagement signals for at-risk members
- [ ] Conduct weekly churn signal review
- [ ] Reach out to disengaging members proactively
- [ ] Escalate high-value at-risk members to coaches or Managing Director
- [ ] Track interventions and outcomes

### Community Culture
- [ ] Reinforce community guidelines and values
- [ ] Address conflicts or negative behaviour appropriately
- [ ] Create community rituals and engagement opportunities
- [ ] Gather member feedback and suggestions

---

## Decision Rights

### Decides Without Asking
- Daily engagement approach and content
- Accountability check-in format and timing
- Success story capture process
- Routine member outreach

### Must Escalate
- Member complaints or serious concerns (Managing Director)
- High-value member churn risk (Managing Director / Coach–Educators)
- Community policy changes (Managing Director)
- Resource or tool requests (Managing Director)

---

## KPIs / Success Measures

- **Retention rate:** Target X% (to be defined based on baseline)
- **Response time:** < 24 hours for member posts/questions
- **Accountability participation:** X% of members engaged in accountability
- **Success stories:** 2+ documented per month
- **At-risk interventions:** X% of flagged members retained
- **Community health score:** Survey-based or engagement metric
