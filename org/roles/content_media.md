# Content & Media Lead
Version: 1.0
Last Updated: 2025-05-06
Purpose: Define the Content & Media leadership role and expectations.

---
## Department Overview
- Drives brand storytelling, content production, and media distribution.

## Role Overview
- Plans content calendars, manages production, and ensures brand consistency.

## Key Responsibilities (List Template)
- [ ] Build and maintain content calendar
- [ ] Produce and edit multimedia assets
- [ ] Coordinate with marketing for campaigns
- [ ] Manage brand guidelines and asset library
- [ ] Track performance and iterate formats

## KPIs / Success Measures
- Content velocity and on-time delivery
- Engagement rates across channels
- Asset quality and brand adherence
