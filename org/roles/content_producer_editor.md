**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-13
**Purpose:** Define the Content Producer / Editor role scope, decision rights, and success measures for Oracle Boxing.

---

| Attribute | Value |
| --- | --- |
| **Current Holder** | G |
| **Primary Department** | Content |
| **Reports To** | Managing Director (Founding Partner) |
| **Collaborates With** | Content Creator (Oliver), Content Manager (G – same person in interim) |

---

## Scope

Edit all video content (long-form YouTube videos, vlogs, short-form content) to professional quality. Optimise edits for retention, engagement, and brand consistency. Deliver polished videos ready for publishing.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### Long-Form Editing
- [ ] Edit 2 YouTube videos per week (8 per month)
- [ ] Optimise edits for high retention and engagement
- [ ] Maintain consistent brand style and quality standards
- [ ] Deliver edited videos on agreed schedule

### Vlog Editing
- [ ] Edit weekly vlog content
- [ ] Maintain vlog style consistency
- [ ] Deliver vlog edits on schedule

### Short-Form Content
- [ ] Repurpose long-form footage into shorts (2 per week minimum)
- [ ] Create additional short-form content as needed (1-2 per week)
- [ ] Optimise short-form for platform requirements (duration, format)

### Quality & Collaboration
- [ ] Participate in weekly edit + presentation review (Saturday)
- [ ] Incorporate feedback from Content Creator
- [ ] Maintain editing workflow and file organisation
- [ ] Communicate timeline and capacity proactively

---

## Decision Rights

### Decides Without Asking
- Editing approach and pacing within brand guidelines
- B-roll and visual element selection
- Audio mixing and enhancement
- Short-form clip selection from long-form footage

### Must Escalate
- Significant timeline delays (Content Creator / Managing Director)
- Major quality concerns with raw footage (Content Creator)
- Capacity constraints affecting output (Managing Director)
- Requests for new content types or formats (Managing Director)

---

## KPIs / Success Measures

- **Long-form output:** 2 edited videos per week delivered on schedule
- **Vlog output:** Weekly vlog edited and delivered
- **Short-form output:** 3-4 shorts per week
- **Turnaround:** Videos delivered within agreed timeline
- **Quality:** Edits approved without major revisions
- **Review participation:** Weekly Saturday review attended
