## Department Overview
- Owns curriculum design, coaching standards, and athlete outcomes.

## Role Overview
- Leads coaching product roadmap, delivery quality, and feedback-driven improvements.

## Key Responsibilities (List Template)
- [ ] Maintain coaching curriculum and update cadence
- [ ] Train and QA coaching team
- [ ] Track athlete outcomes and feedback loops
- [ ] Coordinate launches with marketing and content
- [ ] Own tooling for delivery and measurement

## KPIs / Success Measures
- Curriculum release velocity and adoption
- Session quality scores and completion rates
- Athlete satisfaction/NPS and retention
