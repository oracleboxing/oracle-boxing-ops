# Product & Coaching Team
Version: 1.1
Last Updated: 2025-05-07
Purpose: Outline responsibilities and success metrics for the Product & Coaching function.

---
## Department Overview
- Owns course creation, pricing, payment infrastructure, and delivery of live coaching across Oracle Boxing's offers.

## Role Overview
- Jordan: Sets pricing and builds payment infrastructure for all products and upsells.
- Ollie: Creates and updates Boxing Master Class and Boxing Roadmap; leads group coaching and leaderboard one-to-ones.
- Tony: Product Lead for the One-to-One Coaching Package; leads group and one-to-one community sessions and graduation calls.
- Charlie: Assistant Community Coach and Video Feedback Specialist; supports one-to-ones and personalised video reviews.

## Key Responsibilities
- Maintain course quality and availability across Master Class, Roadmap, and One-to-One Coaching.
- Deliver live coaching: 9 group sessions/week; 10 community one-to-ones/week; 3 leaderboard one-to-ones/month.
- Run onboarding calls for new customers (30–40/month) and graduation calls for clients not upsold.
- Provide personalised video feedback (10–20/week) and daily community engagement.
- Keep pricing models and payment flows current and reliable for all offers.

## KPIs / Success Measures
- Session delivery adherence (group and one-to-ones) and attendance.
- Onboarding and graduation call completion rates and notes logged.
- Course completion/engagement rates and satisfaction feedback from students.
- Payment flow uptime and error rate for purchases and upsells.
- Turnaround time and quality of personalised feedback videos.

## Notes
- Track deliverables in departmental logs; flag any schedule risks early to marketing and operations.
