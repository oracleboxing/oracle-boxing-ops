## Scope

Deliver boxing coaching through group sessions, 1-1 coaching, video feedback, and community engagement. Drive student results through consistent, high-quality coaching and accountability. Different team members hold this role with varying load allocations.

## Key Responsibilities

### Group Coaching Sessions
- [ ] Punctually host assigned group coaching calls
- [ ] Plan the coaching call at least 10 minutes in advance
- [ ] Deliver engaging, educational coaching content
- [ ] Publish the recording of the call within 24 hours

### 1-1 Coaching
- [ ] Deliver 45-minute 1-1 sessions to leaderboard winners (weekly/monthly)
- [ ] Deliver 45-minute 1-1 sessions to paid package customers
- [ ] Prepare personalised feedback for each session
- [ ] Track session completion and member progress

### Video Feedback (Charlie)
- [ ] Leave weekly video feedback on qualifying member posts
- [ ] Follow criteria: key takeaways, actionables, below 5 minutes
- [ ] Clear feedback queue weekly

### Community Engagement
- [ ] Maintain active presence in community (comments, likes, encouragement)
- [ ] Respond to member questions and posts
- [ ] Model positive community behaviour

### Onboarding (Toni)
- [ ] Deliver 15-minute onboarding call to every new member
- [ ] Set expectations and help members get started
- [ ] Answer initial questions and provide guidance

### Course Creation (Oliver)
- [ ] Create and update high-quality course material
- [ ] Ensure course content is current and effective
- [ ] Incorporate feedback from coaching sessions

## Decision Rights

### Decides Without Asking
- Session content and teaching approach
- Personal coaching style within brand guidelines
- Scheduling of 1-1 sessions within allocated slots
- Feedback approach for video reviews

### Must Escalate
- Schedule changes affecting coverage (coordinate with team)
- Member concerns or complaints (CEO)
- Capacity constraints (CEO)
- Course content strategy changes (CEO / Oliver)

---

## KPIs / Success Measures

- **Session delivery:** 100% of scheduled sessions delivered
- **Punctuality:** Sessions start on time
- **Recording turnaround:** Posted within 24 hours
- **1-1 completion:** All leaderboard/paid sessions delivered
- **Video feedback:** Queue cleared weekly (Charlie)
- **Onboarding completion:** 100% of new members receive call (Toni)
- **Community presence:** Active engagement maintained
