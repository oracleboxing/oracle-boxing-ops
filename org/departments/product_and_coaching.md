## Outcome

Members achieve measurable boxing improvement through structured courses, consistent live coaching, personalised feedback, and supportive onboarding. The coaching experience drives retention and results.

### Group Coaching (9 sessions/week)
| Deliverable | Owner | Cadence | KPI/Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Punctually host 9 boxing group coaching calls for the community per week | Oliver, Toni, Charlie | Weekly | All 9 sessions delivered on schedule |
| Plan the coaching call at least 10 minutes in advance | Oliver, Toni, Charlie | Per session | Pre-call prep completed |
| Publish the recording of the call within 24 hours | Oliver, Toni, Charlie | Per session | Recordings posted within 24 hours |
| Plan 3 core coaching topics weekly, shared across all coaches/time zones | Oliver, Toni, Charlie | Weekly | Topics aligned and consistent |

**Operating Notes:**
- All group coaching calls are hosted natively on Skool (no Zoom)
- All recordings must be posted in Skool under Call Recordings within 24 hours
- Only the most valuable calls will be curated into courses over time
- Each week, we plan 3 core coaching topics shared across all coaches/time zones. Calls still allow flexibility for individual needs

### 1-1 Coaching
| Deliverable | Owner | Cadence | KPI/Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Deliver a 45-minute 1-on-1 coaching call to members who have purchased a 1-on-1 coaching package | Toni | 1-2 per week | Coaching package sessions delivered as purchased |

**Note:** 1-1 coaching is primarily reserved for paid coaching packages. Leaderboard 1-1 rewards are limited and defined by the weekly plan—not guaranteed for all top-10 members. Fast-track 1:1 recordings are not posted to the community to avoid noise and decision fatigue.

### Leaderboard Rewards System
| Deliverable | Owner | Cadence | KPI/Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Run 7-Day Leaderboard (Top 10) and 30-Day Leaderboard | All coaches | Weekly | Leaderboards updated and rewards distributed |
| Run weekly video submissions and accountability | Charlie | Weekly | Submissions reviewed and feedback provided |

**Operating Notes:**
- We run a 7-Day Leaderboard (Top 10) and a 30-Day Leaderboard
- Rewards are structured to incentivise submissions and progress without overextending 1:1 coaching time
- Any leaderboard 1:1 rewards are limited and defined by the weekly plan
