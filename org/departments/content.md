# Content Department

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-13
**Purpose:** Consistent high-quality content pipeline that feeds growth and reinforces brand authority.

---

## People

- **Oliver** – Content Creator (planning, scripting, filming)
- **G** – Content Producer / Editor (editing) + Content Manager (publishing & performance) [interim]
- **Fiverr** – Thumbnails (external vendor)

---

## Outcome

Deliver a consistent pipeline of high-quality YouTube videos, vlogs, and short-form content that attracts new audience members, builds trust, and supports marketing campaigns.

---

### Long-Form Content Production

#### Content Creation (Oliver)
| Deliverable | Owner | Cadence | KPI/Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Script 8 YouTube videos per month (2 per week) | Oliver | Monthly | 8 scripts completed per month |
| Film 8 YouTube videos per month (2 per week) | Oliver | Monthly | 8 videos filmed per month |
| Upload raw YouTube videos to Dropbox and link in content schedule | Oliver | Per video | All raw footage uploaded and linked |

#### Content Management – Publishing & Performance (G)
| Deliverable | Owner | Cadence | KPI/Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
