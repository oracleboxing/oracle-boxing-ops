# Organisational Blueprint
Version: 1.0
Last Updated: 2025-05-06
Purpose: Define Oracle Boxing's mission, departmental structure, people, responsibilities, deliverables, and hiring roadmap.

---
## 1. Company Mission
- Deliver world-class boxing education and coaching that blends elite training methods with digital-first delivery.
- Build a trusted brand that empowers athletes and enthusiasts to progress consistently and safely.
- Operate with operational excellence, measurable outcomes, and repeatable customer success.

## 2. Department Overview
- Product & Coaching: Curriculum design, coaching delivery, athlete outcomes.
- Content & Media: Brand storytelling, social content, video production.
- Marketing & Sales: Growth, funnels, conversion, revenue operations.
- Operations & Finance: Systems, support, compliance, cash discipline.

## 3. Product & Coaching
### People
- Head of Product & Coaching (lead)
- Coaching Specialists
- Curriculum Designer

### Responsibilities
- Maintain coaching playbooks and curriculum roadmaps.
- Ensure consistent delivery quality and session standards.
- Collect and act on athlete feedback; publish improvement cycles.

### Deliverables
- Updated coaching curriculum releases.
- Session quality benchmarks and QA reports.
- Feedback loops and iteration logs.

## 4. Content & Media
### People
- Content Lead
- Video Producer/Editor
- Social Media Manager

### Responsibilities
- Create weekly content calendars aligned to launches.
- Produce high-quality video and written assets.
- Maintain brand guidelines and asset library.

### Deliverables
- Published content calendar.
- Campaign assets and edit-ready masters.
- Brand kit updates and usage notes.

## 5. Marketing & Sales
### People
- Growth Lead
- Paid Media Manager
- Sales/Enrollment Lead

### Responsibilities
- Own demand generation, funnels, and lead nurture.
- Manage paid channels and conversion optimization.
- Run sales process, demos, and enrollment operations.

### Deliverables
- Funnel performance reports and experiments log.
- Campaign briefs and launch retrospectives.
- Sales pipeline health dashboards.

## 6. Operations & Finance
### People
- Operations Manager
- Finance & Admin Lead
- Support Specialists

### Responsibilities
- Keep systems, automations, and support running reliably.
- Manage invoicing, payroll, VAT filings, and reconciliations.
- Enforce data hygiene, security, and compliance practices.

### Deliverables
- Support SLAs and resolution reports.
- Monthly close and cashflow updates.
- Automation runbooks and incident logs.

## 7. Hiring Roadmap
- Near-term: Support Specialist, Social Media Manager.
- Mid-term: Additional Coaching Specialist, Lifecycle Marketer.
- Long-term: Revenue Operations Lead, Finance Controller.
