# Cashflow Planning
Version: 1.0
Last Updated: 2025-05-06
Purpose: Guide cashflow forecasting, monitoring, and decision-making.

---
## Overview of the Department
- Finance tracks liquidity, scenarios, and runway to inform decisions.

## Process Steps
1. Update starting balances and receivables/payables
2. Refresh revenue and expense forecasts
3. Model scenarios (base, upside, downside)
4. Review variances vs prior forecast
5. Decide actions (spend controls, timing adjustments)
6. Communicate summary to leadership

## KPIs / Checks
- Runway months and burn multiple
- Forecast vs actual variance
- Cash-in timing vs plan
