# Payroll Process
Version: 1.0
Last Updated: 2025-05-06
Purpose: Document payroll preparation, approvals, and payment steps.

---
## Overview of the Department
- Finance manages payroll accuracy, compliance, and timely payments.

## Process Steps
1. Collect timesheets/comp changes
2. Validate gross pay, deductions, benefits
3. Run payroll preview and QA
4. Approvals (finance + leadership)
5. Execute payments and distribute payslips
6. Post-payroll reconciliations and filings

## Controls & Checks
- Access controls on payroll system
- Variance checks vs prior period
- Compliance with tax/benefit rules
