# VAT Process
Version: 1.0
Last Updated: 2025-05-06
Purpose: Outline VAT calculation, filing, and record-keeping steps.

---
## Overview of the Department
- Finance manages VAT compliance, filings, and documentation.

## Process Steps
1. Gather taxable sales and purchase data
2. Validate tax rates and jurisdictions
3. Prepare VAT return and supporting schedules
4. Review and approval
5. File and remit payment
6. Archive documentation and confirmations

## Controls & Checks
- Rate validation
- Reconciliation to GL
- Filing confirmation stored
