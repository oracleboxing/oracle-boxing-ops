## Evaluation Criteria

Before promoting an idea to the roadmap, answer:

1. Does it serve a roadmap objective?
2. What's the expected revenue impact?
3. What's the resource cost (time, money, attention)?
4. What's the risk if it fails?
5. Is the timing right given cash flow?
